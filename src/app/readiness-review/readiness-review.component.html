How do we help businesses?

Data Inventory

Identify and catalog all personal data that your organisation has collected, processed, and stored. This includes documenting where each type of personal data is stored in your systems.

Data Mapping

Map the flow of personal data within your operations and to third-party entities. This helps in understanding how data moves through different departments and external partners.

Legal Basis

Review the legal grounds on which you’ve processed personal data to ensure alignment with PDPL framework. This involves verifying that data processing activities are conducted lawfully.

Privacy Policies

Evaluate existing privacy policies and notices to ensure they meet PDPL disclosure requirements. This includes transparency about data processing purposes and rights of individuals.

Consent Management

Assess how you’ve obtained, recorded, and managed consent for data processing activities. This includes ensuring mechanisms for obtaining explicit consent for sensitive data and enabling easy withdrawal of consent.

Service Overview

Comprehensive Review of Data Processing Activities

We start by conducting an exhaustive review of your organisation's data processing activities. This includes:

  • Mapping out data flows across all departments and functions.

  • Reviewing data collection, storage, processing, and disposal practices.

  • Identifying all personal data touchpoints within your organisation.

Assessment of Current Compliance Status

Our experts will evaluate your current compliance status against PDPL requirements. This involves:

  • Analysing your existing data protection policies and procedures.

  • Reviewing your data security measures and protocols.

  • Assessing your organisation's awareness and training programs related to data protection.

Identification of Compliance Gaps

Through a meticulous assessment, we will identify any gaps in your current practices. This process includes:

  • Highlighting areas where your organisation falls short of PDPL standards.

  • Identifying potential risks and vulnerabilities in your data protection framework.

  • Providing a detailed report that outlines specific non-compliance issues.

Customized Recommendations for Improvement

Based on the identified gaps, we will offer tailored recommendations and automated PDPL tools to help you achieve compliance. Our suggestions will cover:

  • Updates to your data protection policies and procedures.

  • Enhancements to your data security measures.

  • Improvements to employee training and awareness programs.

  • Implementation of best practices for data handling and protection.

Development of a Compliance Action Plan

We will assist you in developing a comprehensive action plan to address the identified gaps. This plan will:

  • Outline clear, actionable steps to achieve PDPL compliance.

  • Include timelines and milestones to ensure timely implementation.

  • Assign responsibilities to relevant stakeholders within your organisation

Ongoing Support and Monitoring

Compliance is an ongoing process, and we provide continuous support to ensure you remain compliant. Our services include:

  • Regular follow-up assessments to monitor your progress.

  • Updates on any changes to PDPL regulations that may affect your compliance status

  • Ongoing advice and support to address any new challenges that arise.

Customizable Tools and Resources

To facilitate effective compliance management, we offer customisable tools and resources, including:

  • A compliance management dashboard for real-time monitoring of your progress.

  • Templates and checklists to streamline your compliance efforts.

  • A collaborative platform for department managers and Data Protection Officers (DPOs) to work together efficiently.

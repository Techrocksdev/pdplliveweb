How do we help businesses?

Initial Assessment and Gap Analysis

We begin with a thorough assessment of your current data protection practices. Our experts will:

  • Conduct an in-depth review of your existing data processing activities.

  • Identify any gaps or areas where your organization may not be fully compliant with PDPL requirements.

  • Provide you with a detailed report highlighting the areas of concern and recommendations for improvement.

Customised Compliance Roadmap

Based on the initial assessment, we will develop a tailored compliance roadmap for your organisation. This roadmap will:

  • Outline the specific steps you need to take to achieve full compliance with PDPL.

  • Include timelines and milestones to help you track your progress.

  • Be customised to fit the unique needs and operations of your organisation.

Policy and Procedure Development

Our team will assist you in developing and implementing robust data protection policies and procedures. We will:

  • Draft clear and comprehensive policies that align with PDPL requirements.

  • Create procedures for data handling, storage, and disposal to ensure your customers' data is protected at all times.

  • Help you establish protocols for data breach response and incident management.

Employee Training and Awareness

Ensuring that your staff is knows about the law is crucial for compliance. We provide

  • Tailored training sessions for your employees, covering the essentials of PDPL

  • Ongoing education and awareness programs to keep your team updated on the latest regulatory changes and best practices.

  • Resources and materials to support continuous learning and adherence to compliance standards.

Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs)

We conduct impact assessments for both new projects and existing data processing activities, to:

  • Evaluate the potential impact on data privacy and identify any risks to your customers' data.

  • Develop mitigation strategies to address identified risks and ensure compliance with PDPL.

  • Provide detailed reports and documentation to support your compliance efforts.

  • Offer a customisable tool for department managers and DPOs to collaborate effectively on mitigating identified risks.

Ongoing Compliance Monitoring and Support

Compliance is an ongoing process, and we are here to support you every step of the way. Our services include:

  • Regular compliance audits to ensure continued adherence to PDPL

  • Monitoring of regulatory updates and changes to keep your policies and practices current.

  • Providing expert advice and support whenever you need it.

Data Subject Rights Management

We help you effectively manage and respond to data subject requests, ensuring your customers' rights are respected. Our services include:

  • Setting up an automated tool for handling access, rectification, and deletion requests.

  • Ensuring timely and compliant responses to data subject inquiries.

  • Providing guidance on complex requests and potential conflicts.
